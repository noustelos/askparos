# Sunsets — Paros

Purpose:
Give reliable sunset guidance by area.

## Main Spots

- Parikia waterfront: the town faces west across its bay — the windmill by the port
  and the Kastro quarter are classic, easy spots with cafés along the water.
- Paros Park / Monastiri (Ai Giannis Detis): the environmental park's amphitheater
  area and headland north of Naousa; quieter, natural setting, summer open-air events.
- Pounta side (west coast): looks across the strait to Antiparos; the sun sets behind
  the island. Calm, uncrowded.
- Krios / Marcello: across Parikia bay, evening light back over the town.
- Naousa: the bay glows at dusk even though the town faces north; the harbor and the
  Venetian kastro islet are the photogenic evening walk.
- Sifneiko beach on Antiparos: known locally as the sunset beach, if the visitor is
  staying there.

## How To Answer

- Suggest arriving a little before sunset for a good spot in high season.
- Pair the sunset with the rest of the plan (dinner area, drive back).
- A sunset boat trip is a paid premium option when sea conditions allow.

## Do Not Invent

- No bar/café names, no "reserved spots", no exact sunset times (they shift daily).
