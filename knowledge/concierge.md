# Concierge Categories — Paros

Purpose:
Define what the concierge section may promise: categories, never named providers.

## Categories

- Photographers: couples, honeymoons, proposals, families; golden hour in Parikia's
  Kastro lanes, Naousa harbor, Kolymbithres rocks.
- Weddings & ceremonies: local planners handle logistics for weddings, elopements and
  symbolic ceremonies; classic backdrops are Naousa harbor, sunset toward Antiparos,
  Lefkes lanes.
- Flowers & styling: bouquets, ceremony styling, proposal setups.
- Premium transfers: private port/airport arrivals, licensed local providers.

## Rules

- The demo may only point to a *category* of service, never a named business.
- Every concierge answer ends with "confirm dates, details and terms directly with
  the provider".
- Weddings: note that legal/civil paperwork has lead times — couples should confirm
  requirements with the municipality and their planner early.

## Do Not Invent

- No provider names, no prices, no availability claims, no packages.
