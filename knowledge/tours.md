# Tours & Boat Trips — Paros

Purpose:
Frame the island's paid experiences honestly.

## Boat Trips

- Paros–Antiparos day cruises: loop both islands with swimming stops in coves without
  road access; many include Despotiko (ancient sanctuary islet) and lunch on board.
  Full-day, paid; meltemi and sea conditions can change routes — confirm with the
  provider.
- Shorter options: sunset cruises from Naousa or Parikia when conditions allow.
- Water taxis: in season, small boats connect Naousa/Parikia area beaches — seasonal
  and weather-dependent.

## Land Experiences

- Lefkes & Byzantine path: self-guided walk Lefkes → Prodromos (~1 hour); best in
  morning or late afternoon in summer.
- Marathi marble quarries: the source of ancient Parian marble; free, unfenced,
  atmospheric — proper shoes.
- Paros Park (Monastiri): trails, coves, open-air summer cinema/events.
- Wine tastings: local wineries offer visits — paid, book ahead.

## Windsurfing / Kitesurfing

- Golden Beach & New Golden Beach: internationally known windsurfing waters; schools
  and rentals for all levels; the meltemi is the engine (July–August peak).
- Pounta (west coast): the kitesurfing hub, with the Antiparos strait's steady wind.
- Santa Maria: water-sports center on lighter days.

## How To Answer

- Flag every organized tour/lesson as a paid experience.
- Always end with "confirm schedule/conditions with the provider".

## Do Not Invent

- No operator names, no prices, no departure times, no discount codes.
