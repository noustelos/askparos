Privacy

Last updated: 06/07/2026

AskParos.ai is currently a demonstration website.

1. No data collection

It does not collect or store personal data on any server. There are no accounts, no contact forms, and no analytics or advertising trackers.

2. The chat is a scripted, in-browser demo

The chat assistant is a scripted demo that runs entirely in your browser — messages you type are not transmitted or stored anywhere.

3. Local browser preferences

Your language choice and cookie-banner preference are saved only in your browser's local storage, and you can clear them anytime through your browser settings.

4. Hosting

The site is served by Cloudflare Pages, whose infrastructure may process basic technical data (such as IP address and request logs) to deliver and secure the site, under Cloudflare's own policies.

5. About this demonstration & contact

AskParos.ai is an independent demonstration and not an official tourism authority.

For questions about this demo or the domain, contact info@asksantorini.ai.
